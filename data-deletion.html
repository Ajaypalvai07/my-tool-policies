Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: 29/06/2025

Our application does not store any of your data on our servers. All information is used temporarily to perform actions via the Facebook API.

You can remove our app's access and delete any connection by going to your Facebook Settings:

  1. Go to your Facebook profile.
  2. Click "Settings & Privacy" -> "Settings".
  3. In the left menu, go to "Security and Login" and then "Apps and Websites".
  4. Find our app, "Email_magnet", in the list and click "Remove". This will revoke all permissions.
